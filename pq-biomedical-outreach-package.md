# PQ Biomedical Outreach Package — Handover Document

## What We're Selling

**Post-Quantum Biomedical Data Readiness** — consulting engagement assessing post-quantum cryptographic (PQC) risk for organizations handling sensitive biomedical data.

**The 30-second pitch:** Your data must stay secret for 25-50 years (genomic = lifetime, clinical trials = 25 years under EU CTR). Quantum computers are projected within 10-15 years. Nation-state actors are harvesting encrypted data NOW for future decryption ("harvest now, decrypt later"). Your RSA/ECC encryption is already failing in a forward-looking sense. This is a present-day operational risk.

**What we deliver:**
- Cryptographic dependency map of the client's biomedical data infrastructure
- Prioritized migration roadmap from classical to post-quantum cryptography
- Data confidentiality horizon analysis
- Architecture blueprint for PQC-ready infrastructure
- Executive risk briefing translating technical exposure into business/regulatory risk

---

## The Core Argument

GDPR Article 32 requires "state of the art" technical measures. EU Clinical Trials Regulation 536/2014 mandates 25-year data retention. NIST finalized PQC standards (FIPS 203/204/205) in 2024.

**The question for every CRO and pharma company:** When your auditors ask whether RSA-2048 qualifies as "state of the art" for data that must remain confidential until 2051, what is your answer?

No major CRO has a PQC program. Zero. First mover sets the industry standard.
